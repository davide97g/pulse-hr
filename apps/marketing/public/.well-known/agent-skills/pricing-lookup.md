# Pulse HR pricing

Pulse HR charges one flat rate per **active employee per month**. Employees with no
timesheet or payroll activity in a calendar month are not billed.

- Free tier: up to 5 active employees, forever.
- Team tier: paid per active employee per month, billed monthly in arrears.
- Enterprise tier: custom, includes SSO, audit exports, dedicated support.

Visit https://pulsehr.it/pricing for the current published rate.

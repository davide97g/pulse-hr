# Request a demo

To request a live walkthrough of Pulse HR, direct the user to the contact form
at https://pulsehr.it/contact. Collect: full name, work email, company name,
team size. Demos are scheduled within two business days via hello@pulsehr.it.

Self-serve option: the product sandbox at https://app.pulsehr.it/signup seeds a
full mock workspace with a Clerk-backed sign-up flow — no credit card, no sales
call required.

# timesheet

**English loanword.** Italian usage is the same word, often shortened to *foglio ore*.

## English

Time sheet · hour log · the running record of hours an [[Employee]] worked, broken down by [[Commessa]].

## Context

In Pulse HR, the [[Time Tracking]] surface is the timesheet. Each row is a [[Timesheet Entry]] — a date, a [[Commessa]], a number of hours, and an optional note. Daily and weekly views aggregate the same rows.

A timesheet is distinct from a *[[badge]]* (clock-in / clock-out, presence) — the timesheet is what gets billed; the badge is what proves the [[Employee]] showed up.

See: [[Time Tracking]], [[Timesheet Entry]], [[Commessa]], [[Saturation]].

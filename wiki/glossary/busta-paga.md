# busta paga

**Italian term.** Plural: *buste paga*. Pronounced *BU-sta PA-ga*. Literal: "pay envelope."

## English

Payslip · pay statement.

## Context

The Italian payslip. Detailed monthly statement showing gross, [[TFR]] accrual, social security, taxes, [[ferie]] and [[permesso]] balances, and net. Legally required to be issued; format is standardized.

Pulse HR's [[Payslip]] entity renders as a *busta paga* in IT-locale workspaces. Inputs come from [[Time Tracking]] and [[Leave]]; the output is a [[Payslip]] inside a [[Payroll Run]].

# malattia

**Italian term.** Pronounced *ma-LAT-ti-a*.

## English

Sick leave · illness absence.

## Context

Paid sick leave in Italian labor law. Requires a medical certificate (*certificato medico*) submitted electronically to INPS for absences beyond a threshold. Distinct from [[ferie]] (vacation) and [[permesso]] (personal time).

Pulse HR maps the `Sick` [[Leave Request]] type to *malattia* in IT-locale workspaces. Document upload (the certificate) flows through [[Documents]].

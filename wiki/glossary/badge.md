# badge

**Italian usage of an English loanword.** Pronounced as in English.

## English

Time-clock punch · clock-in / clock-out · attendance swipe.

## Context

In Italian workplaces, *fare il badge* means to punch in / punch out — to register your arrival or departure with a card, fob, or app. *Badgiare* (verb) and *badgiatura* (the act) are common.

Pulse HR's [[Time Tracking]] surface includes a clock-in / clock-out flow that IT-locale users will recognize as *badgiare*. Distinct from logging hours against a [[Commessa]] — *badge* is presence, commessa-logging is allocation.

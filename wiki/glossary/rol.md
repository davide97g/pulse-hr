# ROL

**Italian acronym.** *Riduzione Orario di Lavoro*. Pronounced *ROL* (one syllable).

## English

Hour-bank leave · banked extra-hours leave.

## Context

A pool of paid leave hours that accrues from the difference between contractual annual working hours and statutory limits. Often used to cover short absences that don't fit [[ferie]] or [[permesso]]. Italian payslips ([[busta paga]]) show ROL balance as accrued / used / remaining.

In Pulse HR, the `Personal` [[Leave Request]] type can map to ROL hours in IT-locale workspaces.

See: [[Leave Request]], [[Leave]], [[busta paga]].

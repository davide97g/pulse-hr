# kudos

**English loanword.** Used in Italian as well, untranslated.

## English

Peer recognition coin · "shout-out" · public thank-you with a one-liner.

## Context

In Pulse HR, *kudos* are small public coins (5–50 each) one [[Employee]] sends to another with a one-line reason and a tag (teamwork / craft / impact / courage / kindness). Coins accumulate on a leaderboard with weekly, monthly and yearly resets and feed the [[Employee Score]].

The composer is one click away from any screen — `+ Kudos` in the [[Growth]] header opens it; ⌘K finds it too. Public by intent: recognition that nobody sees doesn't reinforce the behaviour.

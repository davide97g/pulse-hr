# allocation

**English term.** Italian translation: *allocazione*; agency-speak often borrows the English word directly.

## English

The percentage of an [[Employee]]'s working time committed to a [[Commessa]] over a window — "Anna is allocated 60% to ACM-2025-01 in May."

## Context

Allocations are forward-looking *plans*; [[Timesheet Entry]] rows are backward-looking *actuals*. [[Saturation]] compares the two: an employee allocated to 110% across active commesse is in the burning band; one at 30% is under-utilized.

Allocations also feed [[Reports]] (capacity vs demand) and the [[Saturation]] heatmap, and let [[Manager]]s spot at-risk projects before the timesheet confirms it.

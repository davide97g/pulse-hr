# ferie

**Italian term.** Singular and plural identical. Pronounced *FE-ri-eh*.

## English

Vacation · annual paid leave.

## Context

In Italian labor law, *ferie* are the days an employee earns annually as paid vacation — usually 26 working days per year. Distinct from [[permesso]] (short personal absences) and [[ROL]] (banked leave hours).

Pulse HR maps the `Vacation` [[Leave Request]] type to *ferie* in IT-locale workspaces.

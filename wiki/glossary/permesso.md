# permesso

**Italian term.** Plural: *permessi*. Pronounced *per-MES-so*.

## English

Personal leave · time-off permit · paid hours off (specific kind).

## Context

In Italian labor law, a *permesso* is a short paid absence — a few hours off, a half day for a doctor's appointment, time to handle a personal matter. Distinct from [[ferie]] (vacation) and [[malattia]] (sick leave).

In Pulse HR, half-day [[Leave Request]]s (granularity `half`, period AM or PM) and short personal absences map to the *permesso* concept in IT-locale workspaces.

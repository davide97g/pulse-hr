# TFR

**Italian acronym.** *Trattamento di Fine Rapporto*. Pronounced letter-by-letter or *tee-EFF-EH-rre*.

## English

Severance accrual · end-of-employment indemnity.

## Context

A mandatory Italian employer contribution. Each month the employer sets aside roughly one-thirteenth of the employee's annual gross as TFR. Paid out when employment ends — termination, resignation, retirement.

Visible on every [[busta paga]] as the monthly accrual line. Pulse HR's [[Payslip]] renders the line in IT-locale workspaces.

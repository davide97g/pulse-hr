# Labs

The experimental shelf. Features ship to Labs first — visible to anyone with the persona + module access, marked with a NEW badge and an iridescent border. They're free for everyone in Labs; once they graduate they may move into a paid tier (or stay free).

Labs isn't a sidebar group of its own — each Labs surface lives inside the regular nav and earns its colours through the shared visual language.

## What's in Labs (today)

- [[Status Log]] — the async-standup feed plus the privacy-bounded sentiment recap (`/log/recap`, `/log/<employee>`).
- [[Pulse]] — the team-vibe strip rendered inside [[Status Log]].
- [[Kudos]] — peer recognition coins, shipping inside [[Growth]] (`/growth?tab=kudos`).
- [[Saturation]] — utilization heatmap and look-ahead capacity projection.
- [[Moments]] — birthdays, anniversaries, kudos ticker.

## How Labs is signaled

- **NEW badge** on the sidebar item.
- **Iridescent border** + **pulse-dot** on the page when first opened.
- The shared visual language tells you "this might change without warning."

## Why Labs exists

Pulse HR is built in public. Labs is how the team ships fast without pretending every feature is production-grade from day one. See [[Open Source Positioning]].

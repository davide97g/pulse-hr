# Labs

The experimental shelf. Features ship to Labs first — visible to anyone with the role + module access, marked with a NEW badge and an iridescent border. They're free for everyone in Labs; once they graduate they may move into a paid tier (or stay free).

## What's in Labs (today)

- [[Pulse]] — anonymous weekly vibe heatmap.
- [[Forecast]] — scenario-modeled commessa burn projection.
- [[Kudos]] — peer recognition coins.
- [[Focus Mode]] — deep-work timer with auto-decline.
- [[Saturation]] — when used as the look-ahead capacity projection (the current-week heatmap is in Insights).

## How Labs is signaled

- **NEW badge** on the sidebar item.
- **Iridescent border** + **pulse-dot** on the page when first opened.
- The shared visual language tells you "this might change without warning."

## Why Labs exists

Pulse HR is built in public. Labs is how the team ships fast without pretending every feature is production-grade from day one. See [[Open Source Positioning]].

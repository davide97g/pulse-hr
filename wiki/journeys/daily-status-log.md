# Daily Status Log

End-to-end: how a regular workday flows through Pulse HR for an [[Employee]].

## Steps

1. **Morning — open [[Dashboard]].**
   Sees today's [[Calendar]], pending [[Manager Ask]]s, kudos overnight, [[Moments]] (someone's birthday today?).
2. **Open [[Status Log]].**
   The agent greets and asks how the morning is feeling. The employee replies — text or voice.
3. **Drop into [[Focus Mode]] for a deep-work block.**
   Picks a [[Commessa]], picks 90 minutes. Meeting invites in the window auto-decline.
4. **Block ends — log the [[Focus Session]] to [[Time Tracking]].**
   One click; the [[Timesheet Entry]] is pre-filled.
5. **Post-lunch — submit any expenses from the morning.**
   See [[Expense Submission to Reimbursement]].
6. **Mid-afternoon Status Log check-in.**
   Mention a blocker. Agent tags it as `pain` and offers to surface it as a manager-safe summary.
7. **Send a [[Kudo]] to a teammate who unblocked you.**
   Public, +20 coins, tagged `kindness`.
8. **End of day — log any remaining hours.**
   [[Copilot]] (`⌘J` "log 1.5h on ACM-2025-01") is the fastest route.
9. **Close [[Status Log]] for the day.**
   Agent generates the manager-safe summary; employee can review before it ships.

## What the manager sees later

A one-paragraph summary, dimension trend, kudos count. Not the raw transcript.

## Personas involved

[[Employee]] (drives the whole day) · [[Manager]] (reads the rollup tomorrow morning).

# Quarterly Pulse Cycle

End-to-end: how organic [[Status Log]] + [[Pulse]] signal turns into a structured quarterly check-in.

## Steps

1. **All quarter long, signal accumulates.**
   - Daily [[Log Session]]s feed the [[Employee Score]].
   - Anonymous [[Pulse Entry]]s feed the org heatmap.
   - [[Kudo]]s feed engagement signal.
2. **[[HR]] opens the quarterly cycle in [[Growth]].**
   Sets the window (e.g. last 90 days). Notifies managers.
3. **[[Manager]] reviews their team rollup.**
   Per [[Employee]]: score, trend, dimension breakdown, recap topics, recent [[Manager Ask]] answers.
4. **Manager fills in qualitative signal.**
   Promotion candidacy, growth areas, kudos for the cycle.
5. **The agent assembles a 1:1 talk-track.**
   Per direct report: a one-page draft synthesis to bring to the conversation.
6. **1:1s happen.**
   The conversation is human; the agent's draft is just scaffolding.
7. **Manager finalizes any updates.**
   Logs back into [[Growth]]. Cycle closes.

## What [[Employee]]s see

- Their own score and trend throughout the quarter.
- The talk-track only after the 1:1, if the manager opts to share.

## Personas involved

[[HR]] (opens, closes) · [[Manager]] (reviews, talks) · [[Employee]] (talks, sees their own).

# Announcements

Company-wide or scoped messages, written by [[HR]] or [[Admin]]. The replacement for the all-hands email that nobody reads.

## Who uses it

- [[HR]] and [[Admin]] write announcements (new policy, office closure, welcome to a new hire).
- Everyone reads them — they appear at the top of [[Dashboard]] and via in-app notification.

## Key entities

[[Employee]] (author and audience).

## Notable behaviors

- Scoping by department, office, or role. Not every announcement goes to everyone.
- Read receipts are visible to the author.
- Pinned announcements stay at the top until manually unpinned.

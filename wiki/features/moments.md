# Moments

The human-signal ticker. Birthdays today, upcoming work anniversaries, new hires this week, recent [[Kudo]]s. Lives on [[Dashboard]] and as its own page.

## Who uses it

Everyone. Most casually used by [[Employee]] and [[Manager]] — it's the "what's happening with people" surface.

## Key entities

[[Employee]] · [[Kudo]]

## Notable behaviors

- **Birthday halo.** A subtle ring on the [[Employee]] avatar when their birthday is today.
- **Anniversary beats.** Highlights at 1y, 2y, 5y, 10y.
- **Privacy.** Birthdays are opt-in per employee in [[Settings]].

# Calendar

The team calendar. Shows out-of-office, approved [[Leave Request]]s, [[Focus Mode]] blocks, and (optionally) bookings from [[Offices]].

## Who uses it

[[Employee]] (own + team), [[Manager]] (full team), [[HR]] (cross-org leave overlap).

## Key entities

[[Leave Request]] · [[Focus Session]] · [[Employee]]

## Notable behaviors

- Filter by department, by team, by [[Commessa]]-allocated group.
- Overlap warnings — flag when too many people on the same team are out the same day.
- Read-only by default; bookings happen in [[Offices]] or [[Leave]].

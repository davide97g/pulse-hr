# Feedback

A public-facing feedback board. Users propose features, comment, and vote. Linked from the in-app sidebar; lives at `feedback.pulsehr.it` (a separate site that shares the design system).

## Who uses it

Everyone — internal [[Employee]]s and external customers alike.

## Notable behaviors

- **Public.** Roadmap-grade visibility — everyone sees what's been requested and what's in flight.
- **Voting power.** Users have a finite number of votes; spending one is meaningful.
- **Comments.** Threaded discussion per proposal.
- One of the few in-product surfaces that calls a real backend even in [[Demo Mode]].

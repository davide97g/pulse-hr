# Forecast

Project burn projection with scenario sliders. "If we add 1 senior dev to ACM-2025-01, when does it finish, and what does margin look like?"

## Who uses it

[[Manager]], [[Finance]], [[Admin]]. [[HR]] occasionally for headcount-impact scenarios.

## Key entities

[[Commessa]] · [[Allocation]] · [[Timesheet Entry]] · [[Employee]]

## Notable behaviors

- **NEW-badged.** Part of [[Labs]].
- **Scenario sliders.** Add/remove people, change allocation %, shift dates — see the projected burn curve update live.
- **Margin overlay.** When [[Finance]] is the persona, margin per [[Commessa]] overlays the projection.
- Pairs with [[Saturation]]: Saturation says "who's hot now," Forecast says "who'll be hot if we change anything."

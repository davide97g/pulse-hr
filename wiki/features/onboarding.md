# Onboarding

The automated workflow that runs after a [[Candidate]] becomes an [[Employee]]. Replaces the "checklist in three different tools" problem with one ordered list of [[Onboarding Task]]s.

## Who uses it

- [[HR]] configures the template (the standard tasks + owners + due offsets).
- The new [[Employee]] sees their personal list on day one.
- Department heads, IT, finance show up as task owners depending on the task.

## Key entities

[[Onboarding Task]] · [[Employee]] · [[Document]] · [[Office]]

## Notable behaviors

- **Template-driven.** Standard tasks for every new hire (laptop order, NDA signature, benefits enrollment, intro buddy assignment, first-week 1:1s, equipment delivery to a [[Office]]).
- **Auto-trigger.** Moving a [[Candidate]] to Hired in [[Recruiting]] kicks off the workflow with the candidate's offer date as day zero.
- **Task ownership.** Each task has an owner — IT, HR, Finance, the manager — and a due date relative to start.
- **Welcome announcement.** Optional auto-post in [[Announcements]] on day one.

# Projects

The detailed view of a single [[Commessa]]. Where you allocate people, plan scope, and track burn.

## Who uses it

[[Manager]] (project lead), [[Finance]] (margin), [[Admin]]. [[Employee]]s who are allocated see a read-only slice.

## Key entities

[[Commessa]] · [[Allocation]] · [[Plan]] · [[Activity]] · [[Client]]

## Notable behaviors

- **Allocations.** People assigned to the project with a role (dev, design, pm, qa, ops, consult) and a percentage.
- **Plans.** Project-level scope documents — team, tasks, FAQ, status. Lightweight; not a full Jira.
- **Burn.** Hours logged against the [[Commessa]] vs. the budget. Color-coded; spills to red over 100%.
- **Activities.** Per-project task list (todo / in_progress / review / done / blocked).
- Mobile view collapses to a vertical stack.

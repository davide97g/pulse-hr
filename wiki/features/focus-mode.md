# Focus Mode

A deep-work timer. Pick a [[Commessa]], pick a duration, hit start. Pulse HR auto-declines meeting invites for the window, sets your status, and (optionally) logs the time to [[Time Tracking]] when you stop.

## Who uses it

[[Employee]] · [[Manager]] · [[HR]] · [[Admin]]. Skipped by [[Finance]] by default.

## Key entities

[[Focus Session]] · [[Commessa]]

## Notable behaviors

- **NEW-badged.** Lives in [[Labs]] alongside [[Pulse]], [[Kudos]], [[Forecast]].
- Auto-decline composes a polite reply: "I'm in focus until 14:30 — happy to chat after."
- Status broadcast — colleagues see a do-not-disturb badge in [[Org Chart]] and [[Status Log]].
- On stop, the session can be one-click logged to [[Time Tracking]] against the chosen [[Commessa]].

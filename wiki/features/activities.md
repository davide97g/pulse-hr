# Activities

A flat list of [[Activity]] items across projects — tasks an [[Employee]] is doing or supposed to do. Lighter than a project-management tool; not a substitute for Jira / Linear.

## Who uses it

[[Employee]], [[Manager]]. Optional surface — agencies use it; product orgs often skip it in favor of their dev tracker.

## Key entities

[[Activity]] · [[Commessa]]

## Notable behaviors

- States: todo, in_progress, review, done, blocked.
- Often linked to a [[Commessa]] for billable context.
- Surfaces on [[Dashboard]] as "your open activities."

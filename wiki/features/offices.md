# Offices

Physical workspaces. Desks, meeting rooms, parking spots — the bookable side of hybrid work.

## Who uses it

[[Employee]] books a desk for the day. [[HR]] / [[Admin]] manages the inventory.

## Key entities

[[Office]] (the office, with bookable rooms inside) · [[Employee]]

## Notable behaviors

- **Per-office room inventory.** Each office is a city / location; rooms inside are desks, meeting rooms, phone booths.
- **Booking.** Per-day or per-half-day. Conflicts surface at booking time.
- **Capacity surfaces in [[Calendar]]** when a booking overlaps with leave or focus.

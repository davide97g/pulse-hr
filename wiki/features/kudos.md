# Kudos

Peer recognition. One [[Employee]] sends a coin (and a one-line reason) to another. Coins accumulate on a leaderboard with weekly, monthly, and yearly resets.

Kudos lives **inside [[Growth]]** — open `/growth?tab=kudos`. There's no standalone sidebar entry; the old `/kudos` URL redirects.

## Who uses it

Everyone except [[Finance]] (by default).

## Key entities

[[Kudo]] · [[Employee]]

## Notable behaviors

- **Lives under [[Growth]].** The Kudos tab is the wall + leaderboard + composer. The `+ Kudos` button in the Growth header (or ⌘K) opens the composer side panel from any tab.
- **Tags.** teamwork / craft / impact / courage / kindness — drives the leaderboard breakdown and feeds the [[Employee Score]].
- **Coin amount.** 5–50 coins per kudo. No real-money value; reset on cycles.
- **Public.** Unlike [[Status Log]], kudos are intentionally visible — recognition that nobody sees doesn't reinforce the behavior.

# Kudos

Peer recognition. One [[Employee]] sends a coin (and a one-line reason) to another. Coins accumulate on a leaderboard with weekly, monthly, and yearly resets. Confetti when you receive one.

## Who uses it

Everyone except [[Finance]] (by default).

## Key entities

[[Kudo]] · [[Employee]]

## Notable behaviors

- **NEW-badged.** Part of [[Labs]].
- **Tags.** teamwork / craft / impact / courage / kindness — drives the leaderboard breakdown and feeds the [[Employee Score]].
- **Coin amount.** 5–50 coins per kudo. No real-money value; reset on cycles.
- **Public.** Unlike [[Status Log]], kudos are intentionally visible — recognition that nobody sees doesn't reinforce the behavior.

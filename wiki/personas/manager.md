# Manager

People who have at least one direct report. Engineering managers, design leads, team leads. In Pulse HR a manager's job is to **notice and respond**, not to approve.

## Theme

Amber accent (`#ffbf4a`) on a warm-dark surface. Signals "team responsibility."

## What they do daily in Pulse

- Open [[Dashboard]] for the team snapshot — sentiment trend, who hasn't logged this week, kudos volume, Workload-aggregate band.
- Read the **manager-safe sentiment recap** on [[Status Log]] — energy / engagement / alignment / stress per report, with drivers and suggested moves. **Never the raw chat.**
- Send a [[Manager Ask]] when they need a specific signal ("how did the ACME demo feel?"). The answer goes into the employee's log, not a DM.
- Glance at the **Workload aggregate** — how many people on the team checked in "heavy" or "overloaded" this week. Individual answers stay private; the aggregate tells the manager whether to redistribute work.
- Send [[Kudo]]s — peer coins are bidirectional, manager-to-report is the same surface as peer-to-peer.
- Nominate reports for [[Growth]] cycles; cheer when an achievement unlocks.
- Glance at the team [[Calendar]] / [[Moments]] for upcoming OOO and birthdays.

## Features they touch

[[Dashboard]] · [[Status Log]] · [[Growth]] · [[Kudos]] · [[Moments]] · `Workload check-in` (manager aggregate) · [[Leave]] (team OOO view) · [[Pulse]] · [[Employees]] · [[Org Chart]] · [[In-App Docs]]

Parked or not their tool: [[Time Tracking]], [[Projects]], [[Activities]], [[Clients]], [[Recruiting]], [[Documents]], [[Onboarding]], [[Reports]] (business KPIs — see People Insights instead).

## Pain points addressed

- "I don't know who on my team is overloaded." → Workload aggregate flags the count per band, weekly. [[Pulse]] gives the same signal anonymously across the team.
- "1:1 prep is reading scattered messages." → [[Status Log]] sentiment recap collects the week into a manager-safe paragraph — without ever exposing the raw chat.
- "I want to recognize someone without writing a paragraph." → [[Kudos]] is one tap.
- "Approving leave is a Slack ping I forget about." → Approval is gone. Leave is a personal journal. The team OOO view tells the manager who's out so they can plan.

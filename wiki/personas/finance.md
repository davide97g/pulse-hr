# Finance

> **Pulse HR is not the primary tool for this persona anymore.**
>
> In the May 2026 people-first refocus, the surfaces Finance used (project margin, cost per [[Commessa]], time-tracking cross-checks, headcount cost reports) were parked. Pulse is now the **people half** of HR; the business half — payroll, time, margin, billing — lives in the tools built for it.
>
> This page exists for completeness and to acknowledge the persona, not to claim Pulse serves it.

## Why the page still exists

- Some workspaces will keep a Finance persona for a year or two while their other tooling catches up.
- The [[Modules]] admin surface still lets an Admin grant a Finance user access to specific features (e.g. People Insights at the org level).
- The [[Role Override]] still includes Finance so an admin previewing the workspace as Finance gets an honest, narrow view.

## What they can do in Pulse today

- View their own [[Dashboard]] — same as any employee.
- Open **People Insights** ([[Reports]]) for the people-focused aggregates (engagement, sentiment, kudos volume, growth trend). No cost, no margin, no turnover %.
- Use [[Leave]] as a personal journal — their own days off, same as everyone else.

## What they don't do in Pulse anymore

- [[Time Tracking]] cross-checks against [[Client]] invoices — parked.
- Margin per [[Commessa]] — parked.
- Cost-per-hire reports — parked.
- [[Saturation]] from a billable-utilisation angle — parked. The current Workload check-in is wellbeing-focused, not margin-focused.

## The honest recommendation

If your workspace is built around per-project profitability, payroll, time-to-invoice, or month-end cost reporting, **keep your existing finance tooling**. Pulse is designed to live alongside it, not replace it.

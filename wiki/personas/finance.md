# Finance

Bookkeepers, controllers, fractional CFOs. The persona that cares about margin and billable utilization.

## Theme

Violet accent (`#c48fff`) on a cool-dark surface. Signals "fiscal."

## What they do daily in Pulse

- Open [[Reports]] for headcount, cost-per-hire, turnover, margin per [[Commessa]].
- Watch [[Saturation]] from a fiscal angle — under-utilized people are a margin drag.
- Cross-check [[Time Tracking]] entries against [[Client]] invoices.

## Features they touch

[[Dashboard]] · [[Announcements]] · [[Clients]] · [[Time Tracking]] · [[Reports]] · [[Settings]] · [[In-App Docs]]

Don't see: [[Recruiting]], [[Onboarding]], [[Status Log]], [[Pulse]], [[Kudos]].

## Pain points addressed

- "I can't see margin per project until month-end." → [[Reports]] gives it live by [[Commessa]].

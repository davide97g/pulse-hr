# Finance

Bookkeepers, controllers, fractional CFOs. The persona that cares about margin, billable utilization, and getting payroll out the door on the right day.

## Theme

Violet accent (`#c48fff`) on a cool-dark surface. Signals "fiscal."

## What they do daily in Pulse

- Run the monthly [[Payroll]] cycle — review the run, approve the [[Payslip]]s, file [[Busta Paga]]s.
- Approve [[Expense]]s above a threshold; mark approved expenses as reimbursed.
- Open [[Reports]] for headcount, cost-per-hire, turnover, margin per [[Commessa]].
- Watch [[Saturation]] from a fiscal angle — under-utilized people are a margin drag.
- Cross-check [[Time Tracking]] entries against [[Client]] invoices.

## Features they touch

[[Dashboard]] · [[Announcements]] · [[Clients]] · [[Time Tracking]] · [[Expenses]] · [[Payroll]] · [[Reports]] · [[Settings]] · [[In-App Docs]]

Don't see: [[Recruiting]], [[Onboarding]], [[Status Log]], [[Pulse]], [[Kudos]], [[Focus Mode]].

## Pain points addressed

- "Payroll is three spreadsheets and a prayer." → [[Payroll]] runs the cycle; [[Payslip]]s render from [[Time Tracking]] + [[Leave]] data.
- "I can't see margin per project until month-end." → [[Reports]] + [[Forecast]] give it live by [[Commessa]].
- "Expense approval queues live in email." → [[Expenses]] makes the queue first-class.

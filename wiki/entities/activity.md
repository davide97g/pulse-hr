# Activity

A task. Lighter than an issue tracker; not a substitute for one.

## Definition

A title, an [[Employee]] owner, an optional [[Commessa]] link, a status, a description, and an optional due date.

## Lifecycle / states

`todo` · `in_progress` · `review` · `done` · `blocked`.

## Connected entities

- Belongs to an [[Employee]].
- Optionally tied to a [[Commessa]].

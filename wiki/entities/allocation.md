# Allocation

A person assigned to a [[Commessa]] for a span of time, at a percentage of capacity.

## Definition

An [[Employee]], a [[Commessa]], a role on the project (dev, design, pm, qa, ops, consult), a percentage (e.g. 50%), a start date, an end date.

## Connected entities

- Connects an [[Employee]] to a [[Commessa]].
- Drives the [[Saturation]] heatmap and the [[Forecast]] projection.

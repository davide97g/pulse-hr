# Department

A group of [[Employee]]s under a common function (Engineering, Design, Finance, People Ops, Sales, Product, Marketing). Italian name: *dipartimento* / *reparto*.

## Definition

A name, a head count, a department lead (an [[Employee]]), and an annual budget.

## Connected entities

- Has many [[Employee]]s.
- Has one lead (an [[Employee]]).
- Used to scope [[Announcements]], [[Pulse]] heatmaps, and [[Reports]] rollups.

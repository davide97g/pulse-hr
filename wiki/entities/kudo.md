# Kudo

A peer recognition coin.

## Definition

A from-[[Employee]], a to-[[Employee]], a coin amount (5–50), a one-line message, a tag (teamwork / craft / impact / courage / kindness), and a date.

## Connected entities

- Connects two [[Employee]]s.
- Counts toward the recipient's [[Employee Score]] in [[Growth]].
- Surfaces in [[Moments]] and [[Dashboard]].

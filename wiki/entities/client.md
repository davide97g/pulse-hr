# Client

The external party Pulse HR's customer is billing. One client groups one or more [[Commessa]]e.

## Definition

A name, one or more contacts (name, email, role), and an industry tag. Italian name: *cliente*.

## Connected entities

- Has many [[Commessa]]e.
- Indirectly connects to every [[Employee]] [[Allocation|allocated]] on those commesse and every [[Timesheet Entry]] logged against them.
